Living Room 1 JORA

3500$1750$

Living room, modern, laminated MDF fronts, which can be adapted according to the space and your preferences.

  • Description

The above ensemble consists of:
1 upper body (W x W x H) 1600 x 320 x 400 (mm)
2 upper bodies (W x A x H) 1200 x 320 x 400 (mm)
1 upper body (W x W x H) 400 x 520 x 1200 (mm)
1 lower body with drawers (L x W x H) 1200 x 520 x 250 (mm)
1 lower body with drawers (W x W x H) 1800 x 520 x 250 (mm)
3 bodies with welded tips: L x W x H 400 x 320 x 2000 mm

The bodies are made of 18mm and 36mm thick Pal, edged with 0.4mm, 1mm or 2mm thick ABS edging.
The fronts are made of 18mm MDF coated with glossy and superglossy acrylic material, and 1mm superglossy ABS edging.

Hardware:
- HT or FGV hinges (optional Hettich or Blum with cushioning)
- slides with standard blum rollers or soft-close King Slide balls
- quality handles, metal or ABS, or push open system (opening/closing by pressing)
