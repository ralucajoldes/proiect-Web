Description:
The Green sofa JORA corner is a piece of furniture that was designed with the most demanding users in mind.The beautiful quilted seat filled with highly elastic foam and springs is responsible for the highest level of comfort.

-The seats are made with mesh, profiled wavy springs and elastic foam.
-Adjustable headrests and armrests.
-Delfon type extension (drawer)
-Space for storage.
-The metal legs, available in 3 colors (chrome, black, gold).
